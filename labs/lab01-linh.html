Two types of elements

There are two types of elements. Elements that have content have an opening tag and a closing. Empty elements do not have content. Empty elements have a closing tag but are ended by a closing bracket, which consists of </tagname>.

There are two types of elements. There are GENERIC elements. They are block level elements and inline elements. Block level elements are stacked vertically. Inline elements are "e;stacked" horizontally. Examples will follow.
